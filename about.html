Who We Are

The Science Behind
Stackly

BioNexus began as a small genomics startup in Boston, MA. Over 15 years, we've grown to become a global biotechnology platform serving over 500,000 patients and partnering with 120+ leading research institutions.

Our interdisciplinary team of molecular biologists, data scientists, clinicians and engineers collaborate daily to push the boundaries of what's scientifically and technologically possible.

We believe precision medicine should be accessible to everyone. Our cloud-native platform democratizes access to advanced genomic tools, bringing the power of personalized medicine to clinics worldwide.

Our Values

What Drives Everything We Do

Our core values guide every experiment, every product decision and every patient interaction.

🔬

Scientific Rigor

Every claim we make is backed by peer-reviewed evidence. We apply the highest scientific standards to our algorithms, datasets and clinical workflows.

🤝

Patient First

Everything we build starts with the patient. We obsess over outcomes, privacy and accessibility to ensure our technology truly benefits the people who need it most.

🌍

Open Innovation

We share our findings through publications, open datasets and API access because we believe scientific progress accelerates when knowledge is shared freely.
